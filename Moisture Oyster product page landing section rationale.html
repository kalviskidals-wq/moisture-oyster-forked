About This Product
Built for eyes that never get a break

Screens cut your blink rate dramatically — starving the ocular surface of moisture. Air conditioning, air travel, and contact lens wear compound the damage. Most people tolerate discomfort they don't have to.

Moisture Oyster delivers instant hydration and long-lasting tear-film stability through a triple-action formula: Glycerin for immediate lubrication, Sodium Hyaluronate for a sustained mucomimetic moisture film, and L-Carnitine to protect corneal cells from osmotic stress.

No benzalkonium chloride, no preservatives. Buffered to physiologic tear chemistry. Dispensed through an Aptar OSD one-way-valve bottle — FDA-cleared multi-dose preservative-free delivery. Safe for daily use with contact lenses.
